About “Vande Mataram”

This song was originally written by a Bengali poet, Bankim Chandra Chatterjee. It was written in Bengali script in the novel Anandmath. The first two verses of the song were adopted as the National Song of India in October 1937 by the Congress Working Committee prior to the end of colonial rule in August 1947. The title ‘Vande Mataram’ means “I praise thee, Mother” or “I praise to thee, Mother”.
